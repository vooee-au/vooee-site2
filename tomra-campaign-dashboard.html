NSW: Age Segmentation Impact
Before vs After 65+ Exclusion
Impact of excluding ages 65+ on key metrics

Key Finding

After excluding ages 65+ on Meta (mid-January onwards), key events dropped 60.5% and engagement rate fell 20%. Cost per LPV decreased, but the 65+ audience was the most engaged on-site, taking more valuable actions like map clicks.

Recommendation

Split ad sets by age group (25-54 and 65+) with tailored creative per segment rather than excluding either group entirely. Preserves reach to younger audiences while maintaining the high-value engagement from older users.
