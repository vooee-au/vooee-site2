What’s in a name?

“Vooee” is a play on VUI — shorthand for voice user interface — reflecting Jeanna’s background in audio and voice technology, and her belief that voice will become a more natural interface for many kinds of work.

The spelling also nods to the Australian bush call “cooee”: a simple voice call that bridges distance without fuss.

Together they speak to the kind of work Vooee is here to do: AI solutions that feel clear, useful and human.

Common questions

Who is Vooee for?

Vooee works with people who have chosen their work with intent, and want to use AI in a way that actually improves it. Not shortcuts, not growth hacks. Just better ways of working for businesses that care about what they put into the world.

How is Vooee different?

We focus on what actually works in your business — not what sounds good in a proposal. That often means doing less, not more.

Do I need to know anything about AI?

Not at all. We work with people at every level of familiarity. Either way, we start with your business, not the technology.

What does a typical engagement look like?

We’ll start with a 30 minute intro call, and then decide if a workshop makes sense or if we can move straight into research and strategy. From there we might look at updating existing proceses, and consider readily available consumer products, bespoke workflow builds, or even something like a voice agent. We also offer ongoing training and support when needed.

What happens during a workshop?

A workshop is a structured working session that looks at your business goals, customers, current workflows, friction points, and where AI may or may not be useful. You leave with a clearer view of the opportunities, the risks, and what a sensible next step could look like. These can be one on one, or with a team, and if you have a specific challenge in mind, we structure the content and sessions to focus on the most valuable discussions
